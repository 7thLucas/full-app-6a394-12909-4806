The Problem

Zimbabwe's best spots are
invisible.

  • Official ZTA registries are siloed — no single discovery layer exists
  • Local restaurants and hidden lodges are completely off the grid
  • Businesses rely on word-of-mouth with no affordable digital presence
  • Tourists in Victoria Falls, Harare, and Bulawayo have no reliable local guide
The Solution

Three data layers.
One platform.

1

ZTA Seed Data

Official registry of tour operators, hotels, national parks, and restaurants — instant credibility at launch

2

Crowdsourced Additions

Locals drop pins, upload photos, and list off-grid spots — earning Points and badges like "Zim Explorer" and "Local Foodie"

3

B2B Owner Portal

Restaurants and lodge owners claim their listing free — update hours, menus, WhatsApp numbers, and promotions
