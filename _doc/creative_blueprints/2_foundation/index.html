Travel Planning Fragmentation

The Problem

Coordinating travel across fragmented platforms wastes time and money. Flights booked on one app, hotels on another, activities scattered across review sites—all with conflicting calendars and no integrated budgeting.

The Solution

Travelers struggle to coordinate flights, accommodations, activities, and budgets across multiple disconnected apps and services, leading to missed deals, scheduling conflicts, and wasted time.

Target Audience

Adventure seekers, business travelers, and family trip organizers who value time savings and transparency

Personal Motivation

Making travel planning as exciting as the journey itself—removing friction so travelers can focus on experiences, not logistics
