Core Features

Three ways to use it

Discover. Contribute. Own your listing. Each mode feeds the others.

🔍

Find Open Spots

Map-based search shows restaurants, lodges, national parks, and tour operators near you — filtered by city, category, and open-now status. Powered by ZTA data and community pins.

Harare Bulawayo Victoria Falls

Add a Spot

Drop a pin on the map, upload a photo, and list any restaurant or travel spot that isn't in the official registry. Every approved submission earns Points and unlocks badges.

Claim Your Listing

Restaurant owners and lodge managers claim their listing for free via the B2B portal — set operational hours, upload a menu PDF, add a WhatsApp contact, and post promotional prices.
