# Zimbabwe Travel Discovery Platform — Product Overview

## What It Is
A three-sided travel discovery platform focused on Zimbabwe, combining official government tourism data, crowdsourced local knowledge, and business self-service tools to create the definitive map of Zimbabwe's restaurants, lodges, hotels, national parks, and tour operators.

## The Problem
Zimbabwe's travel and hospitality landscape is fragmented and poorly catalogued. International tourists can't easily discover verified local spots. Locals have no reliable go-to platform for dining and travel recommendations. Small businesses — restaurants, lodges, tour operators — rely entirely on word-of-mouth with no affordable digital presence. No single platform consolidates official, local, and business-managed travel information for Zimbabwe.

## The Solution — Three Data Layers

### 1. Official Seed Data (ZTA Partnership)
Baseline database sourced from the Zimbabwe Tourism Authority (ZTA) registry, supplemented by open map registry scraping. Covers registered tour operators, hotels, national parks, and restaurants. Initial focus cities: Harare, Bulawayo, and Victoria Falls.

### 2. Crowdsourced Additions ("Add a Spot")
Users — especially locals — drop a pin on a map, upload a photo, and list any restaurant or travel spot not in the official registry. Gamified with a Points system and achievement badges, including "Zim Explorer" and "Local Foodie."

### 3. Owner Verification (B2B Portal)
Restaurant owners and lodge managers claim their listing for free via a dedicated B2B portal. Owners can update: operational hours, menu PDFs, WhatsApp contact numbers, and promotional prices. Creates self-maintaining, always-current data across the platform.

## Users
- **International tourists** visiting Zimbabwe (pre-trip planning and on-the-ground discovery)
- **Local residents** in Harare, Bulawayo, and Victoria Falls (dining, day trips, weekend travel)
- **Business owners** — restaurants, lodges, tour operators (managing and promoting their own listings)

## Geography
Zimbabwe — initial focus on Harare, Bulawayo, and Victoria Falls.

## Positioning
The definitive Zimbabwe travel discovery layer — every hotel, restaurant, national park, and tour operator in one place, kept current by official ZTA data, local contributors, and business owners themselves.

## Brand & Tone
Local-first, community-driven, credible (ZTA-backed), approachable and gamified for locals, trustworthy and comprehensive for tourists.

## MVP Scope
- Map-based discovery of ZTA-registered travel spots across Harare, Bulawayo, and Victoria Falls
- "Add a Spot" crowdsourcing feature with Points and badge system (Zim Explorer, Local Foodie)
- B2B "Claim Your Listing" portal — operational hours, menu PDFs, WhatsApp contact, promotional prices

## Strategic Principles
- ZTA data partnership provides instant credibility and legal legitimacy at launch
- Crowdsourcing drives organic growth and local community engagement
- B2B portal creates self-maintaining data and a clear future premium monetization path
- Three-sided network effect: more spots → more tourists → more businesses claim → better data → more tourists
